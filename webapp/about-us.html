About Us

Hi! My name is Linn, and I’m a huge Eurovision fan. I absolutely love everything about the competition—the music, the spectacular performances, the over-the-top costumes, and the incredible party atmosphere. There’s something so magical about how Eurovision brings people together from all over Europe (and beyond!) to celebrate music, diversity, and creativity. I look forward to it every year, from the national selections to the grand final. Whether it’s the emotional ballads, the wild dance tracks, or the unexpected quirky acts, I enjoy it all. Eurovision isn’t just a contest—it’s an experience, and I wouldn’t miss it for the world!
